# Code Book


## Identifiers

 - subject  - The ID of the test subject

 - activity - The type of activity performed by subjects when the measurements were taken
   - Walking
   - Walking Upstairs
   - Walking Downstairs
   - Sitting
   - Standing
   - Laying
